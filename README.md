Carrot Ears is a surreal Unity 3D project that takes players on a strange journey through the Void. What begins as a peaceful exploration of a mysterious farm quickly transforms into a tense series of trials as players strive to recover their missing ears. This was a submission for BrackeysGameJam2026.1 with theme "Strange Places".
